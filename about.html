About

Quietly building a life where words and code can speak to each other.

I am a student from Kathmandu whose curiosity lives between literature, technology, and thoughtful design.

My voice

I grew up learning to listen closely to city sounds, weather, and the unspoken pauses between thoughts. Poetry became a space where those observations could breathe. Technology became a way to make those feelings visible and shareable.

My work is rooted in sincerity rather than spectacle. I want each experience I create to feel calm, intentional, and emotionally honest.

Crafted with words, curiosity, and quiet mornings.
