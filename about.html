Chapter One

✦ Who I am

Hi, I'm Diya Subedi. I started writing poetry when I was in Class 10. At first, I never imagined those scattered thoughts would one day become something I would share with the world. They were simply pages I wrote whenever my mind felt too loud or my heart had too much to carry.

For me, poetry isn't a hobby. It's the way I understand life. Almost every poem I write begins with something real — a conversation, a goodbye, a memory, a place, or a feeling that refuses to leave.

I don't chase perfect words. I simply try to write honestly enough that someone else might quietly think, "I've felt this too."

Two sides of the same page

❧ Why I write, why I build

✧ Why I write

I'm inspired by poets who taught me that a few simple lines can stay with a person for years. They made me fall in love with words, and that love slowly became my own voice. I began sharing my poems online partly to become more confident, partly to let more people discover the quiet beauty hidden in language.

✧ Why I build

When I'm not writing, you'll usually find me reading, walking through quiet places, taking photographs, or travelling — collecting small moments that often find their way back into my poems. Technology became the way I could shape those moments into something tangible and shareable.

✎ How I make
