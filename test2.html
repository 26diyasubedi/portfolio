✦ About Me

Read the full story →

I grew up in Kathmandu, where the city's rhythm taught me to listen closely to footsteps, rain, and the silence between thoughts. Literature became the place where I could name what I felt. Computers became the place where I could build forms for those feelings, turning curiosity into something tangible.

Poetry and logic do not feel like opposites to me. One helps me understand the human heart. The other helps me shape an idea with structure and clarity. I am learning both with equal seriousness.
